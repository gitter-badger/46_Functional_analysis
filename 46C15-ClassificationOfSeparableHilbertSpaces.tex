Let $H_1$ and $H_2$ be infinite dimensional, separable Hilbert spaces.  Then there is an isomorphism $f:H_1\rightarrow H_2$ which is also an isometry.

In other words, $H_1$ and $H_2$ are identical as Hilbert spaces.
