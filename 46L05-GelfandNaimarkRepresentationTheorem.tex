{\bf The Gelfand-Naimark representation theorem is as follows:}

{\bf Theorem 1.1}

{\em Every $C^*$-algebra is isometrically isomorphic to a norm closed *-subalgebra $\Bset_{nc}(\hilbert)$ of an algebra $\Bset(\hilbert)$ of bounded operators on some Hilbert space $\hilbert$. In particular, every finite dimensional $C^*$-algebra is isomorphic to a direct sum of matrix algebras.}
