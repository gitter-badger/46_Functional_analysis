Gelfand-Naimark representation theorem


The Gelfand-Naimark representation theorem is as follows:

Theorem 1.1

Every C*-algebra is isometrically isomorphic to a norm closed *-subalgebra Bn⁢c⁢(H) of an algebra B⁢(H) of bounded operators on some Hilbert space H. In particular, every finite dimensional C*-algebra is isomorphic to a direct sum of matrix algebras.
